Stock Market Manipulation through Social Media

Welcome!

Since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, there has been a sharp increase in market activity, especially among younger audiences. Interestingly enough, there's been a correlation between stock prices and social media posts, primarily from famous or powerful celebrity figures.

In order to inform younger users and help them understand market volatility and manipulation, we created a visualization to analyze the correlation between Tesla (TSLA) stock prices and Elon Musk's tweets. This visualization also serves as a way to better epxlore the sphere of influences popular social figures can have and the impact on the market.

All communications and data are public information.

Click here to access the visualization

This visualization was built using React v17.0.1, D3.js v6.5.0, Material-UI v4.11.3, and utilized the Twitter API v2.0 and the Yahoo Finance API v4.2 (unofficial endpoint API).
